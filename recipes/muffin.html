A tasty recipe without sacrificing flavor

Vegan muffinsare a delicious and healthy alternative to traditional muffins.
They are made without eggs, dairy, or butter, but still offer a moist, fluffy texture and a satisfying flavor.
Vegan muffins can be made with a variety of ingredients, including plant-based milk, applesauce, flaxseed meal
and bananas. They can be enjoyed for breakfast, snack, or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 100g all-purpose flour
  • 8g baking powder
  • 20g potato starch or 20g unsweetened cocoa powder (for chocolate muffins)
  • 125g plant-based yogurt (soy, almond, coconut, etc.)
  • 40ml corn oil
  • 60g chocolate chips

  2. Combine all the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, cocoa powder or potato starch) in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Gradually add the oil, whisking continuously.
  4. Add the yogurt and mix well.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Fill the muffin liners halfway with the batter.
  7. Bake the muffins at 180Â°C (350Â°F) for 20-25 minutes on the middle rack of the oven.
  8. Check for doneness with a toothpick.
  9. Serve at room temperature as is or with whipped cream.

Vegan Muffins stay soft for several days. It's best kept them in a cake stand or an airtight container.
